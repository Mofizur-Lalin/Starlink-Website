About Starlink Engineering

Strategic engineering solutions for a sustainable future

Our Story

Established in 2008 by a group of passionate engineers, Starlink Engineering Ltd. has grown into a strategic partner for international organizations, telecom operators, corporate enterprises, and government agencies. We are committed to delivering high-quality, on-time, and cost-effective engineering solutions that simplify our clients' operations and add real value.

Starlink is a trusted engineering service provider in Bangladesh, delivering comprehensive, end-to-end solutions across diverse sectors and strategically delivered through following specialized business units:

Solar EPC

Engineering, Procurement & Construction of solar energy solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial applications.

Telecom Engineering

Advanced communication infrastructure development and implementation for major telecom providers.

CME & Interior Decoration

Civil, Mechanical & Electrical solutions along with interior design services for commercial spaces.

Operation & Maintenance

Comprehensive maintenance services ensuring optimal performance of infrastructure and equipment.

Our core expertise spans the project lifecycle, encompassing project development, site surveys, feasibility studies, planning, engineering and design, procurement, construction and installation, commissioning and testing, operation and maintenance, optimization and tuning as well as supplying relevant products and accessories.

Our strength lies in our skilled workforce of 150+ professionals, well-trained to manage complex engineering projects with precision, safety, and sustainability. Our integrated approach and diversified capabilities enable us to serve both the public and private sectors in telecommunications, civil infrastructure, and renewable energy.

Sustainability & Responsibility

At Starlink, we take our social, environmental, and climate responsibilities seriously. We actively support Bangladesh's transition to clean energy by promoting and delivering solar power solutions for industrial, commercial, and remote applications. Our renewable energy initiatives are designed to reduce carbon emissions, enhance energy resilience, and contribute to national climate goals.

As an environmentally conscious company, we integrate eco-friendly practices into every stage of our projects—from planning and design to execution and maintenance. We also invest in local community engagement, skills development, and inclusive employment, ensuring our growth is responsible and equitable.

We believe that sustainable business practices are not just good for the planet, but essential for long-term business success and community development.

Solar EPC Services

Comprehensive solar energy solutions for a sustainable future

About Our Solar EPC Unit

Starlink Engineering is a trusted name in engineering solutions, offering end-to-end Solar EPC (Engineering, Procurement & Construction) services for clients across various sectors. Our Solar EPC unit is committed to delivering high-performance, cost-effective, and sustainable solar energy solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.

Delivery Modes

We offer flexible delivery and financing models to meet diverse customer requirements:

CAPEX Model

Capital Expenditure Model: Customers invest upfront and own the solar system. Starlink provides turnkey EPC services, ensuring smooth installation, grid integration, and after-sales support.

OPEX Model

Operating Expenditure / PPA Model: Starlink invests in and operates the solar system on the client's premises under a long-term Power Purchase Agreement (PPA). Clients pay only for the electricity consumed—zero upfront investment, hassle-free maintenance.

Our comprehensive scope of work ensures that Starlink delivers high-quality, reliable, and efficient solar systems to our valued clients.
